About Me

For 15 years, I've worked in PR and content development, first in communications strategy for real estate and hotel clients, then specialising in arts and culture. I have developed campaigns, led creative projects, contributed research and curatorial assistance to major exhibitions, managed an artist studio and developed publications in the UAE, Saudi Arabia and the UK. A writer, editor and project manager, now I am learning to code. With my new front-end development skills, I hope to develop creative online solutions for artists and culture organsiations, presenting writing and work online in innovative ways. I come from an arts and humanities background, with a Bachelor's in English Language and Literature from The Queen's College, University of Oxford, and I am currently completing an MA in Visual Cultures from Goldsmiths, University of London.
